OATMEAL COCONUT PANTRY COOKIES

Chef Roman, August 24,2018

Prep Time 30 minutes
Cooking Time 9-13 minutes
Skill Level Easy
Makes 24 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup picked brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 eggs

Directions

  1. Toast coconut at 350 F for about 10-15 minutes. Stiross every 5 minutes
  2. Beat sugar, butter, vanilla, and eggs.
  3. Mix all other Ingredients together.
  4. Scoop cookies and press the dough firmly the ensure the shape is held.
